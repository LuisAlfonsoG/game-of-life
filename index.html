Conway's Game of Life

Game Explanation

The Game Of Life was devised by the mathematician John Horton Conway. It consist on a single grid of squares or cells which can be in two states: dead or alive.

The game have two simple rules which determine the evolution of the game:

  • Every live cell dies unless it has 2 or 3 live neighbors
  • Every dead cell becomes alive if it haves exactly 3 live neighbors
